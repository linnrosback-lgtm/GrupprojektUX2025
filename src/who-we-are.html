Our story

Every great company begins with a spark—ours began with a crown. When King Woody first envisioned a place where creativity, chaos, innovation, and questionable decision-making could thrive together, he gathered the only team capable of bringing such a vision to life: himself… in all his many forms.

From day one, the Woody Collective has embraced the idea that diverse perspectives fuel extraordinary results. Whether it’s Business Woody optimizing systems, Crimelord Woody designing with boldness slightly outside the legal spectrum, or Family Man Woody reminding everyone to stay grounded, each Woody brings something uniquely valuable to the table.

As the kingdom grew, so did the challenges—and that’s when the true strength of the Woody Collective revealed itself. With Unhinged Woody fearlessly tackling HR crises that defy explanation, Hinged Woody keeping projects aligned with reality, and Hipster Woody crafting code so obscure it’s practically performance art, our foundation became unshakable. Meanwhile, Mental Woody explored the depths of user behavior, Priest Woody blessed every line of frontend logic, and Rough Woody hammered backend systems into something unstoppable. And when data needed deciphering or bugs tried to sneak past the gates, Tjackis Woody and Westlife Woody stood as our final line of defense.
